Common Causes of Clogged Drains

Before we get into the signs, let's quickly cover some common culprits behind clogged drains:

  • Hair: It's a drain's natural enemy. Hair clumps together and traps other debris.
  • Soap Scum: Over time, soap scum can build up and restrict water flow.
  • Grease and Oil: Never pour grease down the drain! It solidifies and causes major blockages.
  • Food Scraps: Even with a garbage disposal, some food scraps can still clog your drain.
  • Mineral Buildup: Bellingham's water, like most, contains minerals that can accumulate over time.
  • Foreign Objects: Toys, jewelry, or anything else that accidentally goes down the drain can cause a clog.

Signs You Need Drain Cleaning Services

Here are some key indicators that your drain needs professional attention:

  1. Slow Draining: This is the most obvious sign. If water is draining slower than usual, it's a sign of a partial blockage.
  2. Gurgling Noises: Gurgling or bubbling sounds coming from your drains indicate trapped air in the pipes, usually caused by a blockage.
  3. Unpleasant Odors: Foul smells emanating from your drains are often caused by decaying food and other organic matter trapped in the pipes.
  4. Multiple Clogged Drains: If multiple drains are clogged at the same time, it could indicate a problem deeper within your main sewer line.
  5. Water Backup: If water backs up into your sinks or showers when you flush the toilet, it's a clear sign of a major blockage.
  6. Fruit Flies: An increased presence of fruit flies near your drains can signal decaying organic matter.
